Documentary About The Secret Service

As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, there are several documentaries and television programs that provide insight into the United States Secret Service and its operations. Some of these documentaries include: "The Presidents' Gatekeepers" (2013): This documentary series provides an inside look at the lives and responsibilities of White House Chiefs of Staff, including their interactions with the Secret Service. "Inside the U.S. Secret Service" (2017): This documentary offers a behind-the-scenes view of the U.S. Secret Service's work, focusing on its protective mission and training. "The Secret Service" (2013): A documentary that explores the history, roles, and challenges faced by the U.S. Secret Service. "Secret Service Files" (2016): This documentary series delves into the secretive world of the U.S. Secret Service, covering its history, mission, and notable cases.
